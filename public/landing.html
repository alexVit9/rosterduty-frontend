Got questions? We've got answers.

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial? +
No — and you never have to. RosterDuty has a free plan that never expires. You can use it forever with no credit card required. If you need more locations or staff, you can upgrade to a paid plan at any time.
How long does setup take? +
Under 10 minutes for most restaurants. Create your account, add your locations and team members, pick your templates, and you're live. No IT support or technical knowledge required.
Do you provide ready-made checklist templates? +
Yes! 50+ professional templates covering opening and closing procedures, HACCP food safety compliance, kitchen cleaning, bar setup, server sidework, inventory checks, bathroom inspections, and more. Use them as-is or customize every detail to match your standards.
Can I customize checklists for my restaurant? +
Fully. Add, remove, and reorder checklist items. Attach photo requirements, set time windows for task completion, assign tasks by department or individual staff member. Every template is a starting point — the finished checklist is yours.
Does it work for restaurant chains with multiple locations? +
Yes, multi-location support is built in from day one. Manage all your locations from a single dashboard, standardize procedures across sites, and view per-location reporting. The Growth and Enterprise plans are designed specifically for chains and groups.
What happens after the 3-month free trial ends? +
For the first 3 months you get full access to all Corporate features — free. After that, your account automatically moves to the free plan (1 location, 5 staff, 3 templates). You can stay on the free plan forever at no cost, or upgrade to Professional or Corporate at any time to get everything back. We never auto-charge without your consent.
Is there a mobile app for staff? +
Yes. Staff get a dedicated mobile app available on iOS and Android. They activate their account with a simple invite code sent by the manager, then sign in to see their tasks, check off items, add photos and comments. Designed for fast use during a busy shift.
